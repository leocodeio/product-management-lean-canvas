How to Use the Lean Canvas

Import & Export

The Lean Canvas supports multiple ways to save and share your work:

  • Export as PDF: Click the download button in the toolbar and select "Download PDF" to save a high-quality PDF version of your canvas
  • Export as JSON: Select "Download JSON" to save your canvas data in a structured format that can be imported later
  • Import JSON: Click the upload button to load a previously saved JSON file
  • Auto-Save: Your work is automatically saved to your browser every 2 seconds - no need to manually save!

Getting Started

Fill out each section of the canvas by clicking on the text areas. The canvas is designed to help you think through your business model systematically:

  • Start with Problem: Identify the top 3 problems your customers face
  • Define your Solution: List your top 3 features that solve these problems
  • Craft your UVP: Create a unique value proposition that sets you apart
  • Identify Key Metrics: Define how you'll measure success
  • Plan Revenue & Costs: Map out your financial model
